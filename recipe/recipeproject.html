Dorothy's Carrot Juice Recipe

I learned this recipe from my late grandma Dorothy and it is still one of my favorite drinks.

Directions

  1. In a large pot boil the carrots in water on medium heat.
  2. After 15 minutes use a fork to check if the carrots are cooked, the fork should easily break apart the carrots. If not boil it for 10 more minutes.
  3. Let it cool and after being cooled transfer what can be fit; into a blender. Blend it on the highest setting, then transfer it into a jug and keep doing this until everything is blended.
  4. Pour the can of condensed milk into the jug and use a spoon to mix as you go until it is all mixed.
  5. Put in the nutmeg, vanilla extract and Guinness(optional) into the jug and mix until dissolved. You're all done Enjoy!
